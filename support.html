FAQ

Is the app free?
Yes. Verse Weaver is free to download and play. It includes ads served by Google AdMob.

Can I play offline?
Core verse puzzles work offline. Loading ads and opening this page require internet.

How do I reset my progress?
Open the app → Settings → Reset all progress.

Do I need an account?
No account or sign-in is required.
