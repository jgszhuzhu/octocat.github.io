Verse Weaver is an educational puzzle game by Origin Games. We minimize personal data collection. Ads are served by Google AdMob.

Ads are provided by Google AdMob. Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy. We do not sell your personal information.

Children

Educational poetry content for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you are a parent or guardian.

Your Choices

Limit ad tracking or reset your advertising ID in system settings. Reset game progress in the app or uninstall to remove local data.
